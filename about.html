RONINAKS TECHNOLOGIES

Redefining Excellence

Roninaks, the soul and spirit of a team of young entrepreneurs from Kerala is a software design and development company registered in Bengaluru, Karnataka. Founded in March 2018, under the parent company Roninaks Ventures Pvt. Ltd., the company with it's innovative designs, products and services have been constantly on the rise catering to businesses across the globe in multiple domains. Our team, comprising of the finest professionals across the country strives to ensure client and consumer satisfaction through superior quality designs, immersive user experience, innovative products and services.
